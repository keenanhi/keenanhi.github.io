Mix Feedback


Amazing music doesn't start at the mastering stage. That is why we offer Mix Feedback, giving you access to your mastering engineer during your mixing process.

With Mix Feedback, you get the detailed assistance of your mastering engineer with any mix level issues you are having, along with their professional opinion of your mix. This additional stage in the mastering process is a win-win: you get to stay in control and hone your mixing and recording skills, while we get to give you the absolute best master possible.
