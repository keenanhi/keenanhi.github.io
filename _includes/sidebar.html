What is Get Mastered?

Get Mastered is an instructional blog striving to teach audio engineers and musicians alike how to crack open the clandestine art of mastering music through interactive tutorials.

Though the blog is focused on mastering, ocassionally I will delve into related topics, such as recording, mixing, and songwriting. Indulge me.

Who Am I?

My name is Keenan, and I'm a recording hobbyist and musician. I have always been unsure why the mastering profession is so shrouded in mystery, so this website is my way of clearing the fog, and showing you how to get your own tracks to a professional level.

If you think what I'm doing is like super cool, please consider buying me a cup o' joe. A lot of work goes into this thing!
