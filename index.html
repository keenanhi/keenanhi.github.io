What?

My name is Keenan. I have been mixing and mastering music for most of my life, and have always gawked at the high prices typically charged for these services. With the lack of any legitimate low-cost mastering websites online, I decided to put my skills and experience to use and start my own.

I created GetMastered.com to offer musicians an inexpensive way to acheive a professional, high quality sound. Plain and simple.

In an effort to show I really, really mean it, I have decided to offer free, no-strings-attached mastering to any new members of my growing clientele. After this, I charge the lowest rates I possibly can, to pay for a) a place to live b) food to eat and c) coffee.

I just want to make a modest living helping musicians like you who don’t want to break the bank to get mastered. I am confident that you will love what I do to your song, and hope you don’t miss out on this opportunity!

For a limited time, I’m also offering another free master if you refer a friend. Yep. That’s two songs mastered for free. No sleep for me for a while (that’s what the coffee is for). Just have your friend mention your name to me.

Hope to hear you soon,
Keenan
