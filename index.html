What is Get Mastered?

My name is Keenan, and I founded Get Mastered because there is simply nowhere for musicians to get a professional, quality mastering job for under $100 per song. I've been mixing music for years, and I've found it impossible to get mastered professionaly at anything close to an affordable rate.

With Get Mastered, you are not paying less for a "cheaper" mastering job; you're paying a price that's actually fair.

Get Mastered recruits extremely talented up and coming engineers that understand what fair pricing means for independent musicians, and are eager to work for fair wages doing what they love: helping you.
