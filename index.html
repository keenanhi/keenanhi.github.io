What is Get Mastered?

My name is Keenan, and I founded Get Mastered because there is simply nowhere for artists to connect with experienced audio engineers at affordable, realistic prices.

Get Mastered strives to connect artists with experienced audio engineers a price that's actually fair.

Get Mastered recruits extremely talented up and coming engineers that understand what fair pricing means for independent musicians, and are eager to work for fair wages doing what they love: helping you.

In addition to mastering, Get Mastered offers stem mastering, as well as other audio restoration services for podcasters, voiceovers, or anything else audio. Click here for more about us.
