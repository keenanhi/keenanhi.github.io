Looking for your personal mastering engineer?


My name is Keenan, and I created Get Mastered as a way for independent musicians to foster a direct, personal relationship with an experienced audio engineer to make great music, together.

When it comes to mastering, a lot of producers like to say, "you get what you pay for." I like to think of Get Mastered as the exception that makes the rule.

Projects with higher track counts qualify for rates even lower than $39 per song. Contact me for more info.
